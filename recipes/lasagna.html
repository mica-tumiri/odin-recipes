Lasagna Recipe

About Lasagna

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made of stacked layers of pasta, cheese, and sauce. It is often baked in the oven and served hot.

Ingredients
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions to prepare the best lasagna in the world
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with garlic; drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce; repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; uncover and bake for 15 minutes.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
