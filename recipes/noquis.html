About gnocchi

Gnocchi are soft dough dumplings that are typically made from potatoes, flour, and eggs. They are a popular dish in Italian cuisine and can be served with a variety of sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: grated cheese, herbs

Steps to prepare the best gnocchi in the world

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, flour, egg, and salt. Mix until a dough forms.
  2. On a floured surface, roll the dough into long ropes about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Cut the ropes into 1-inch pieces and shape them into small dumplings.
  4. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the gnocchi until they float to the surface.
  5. Remove the gnocchi with a slotted spoon and serve with your favorite sauce.

Enjoy your homemade gnocchi!
