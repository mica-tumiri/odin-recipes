About gnocchi

Gnocchi are soft dough dumplings that are typically made from potatoes, flour, and eggs. They are a popular dish in Italian cuisine and can be served with a variety of sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: grated cheese, herbs
